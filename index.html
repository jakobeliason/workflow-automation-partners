FAQ

Questions owners ask us.

What does "90-day guarantee" actually mean?+

We scope every engagement to a specific, measurable time-saved target — for example, "recover 20 hours per week across the ops team." If we haven't hit that target within 90 days of go-live, we refund the engagement fee. Simple as that.

Do I need technical staff to use what you build?+

No. Everything we deploy either runs fully in the background or runs in tools your team can learn in an afternoon. If a non-technical manager can't maintain it, we haven't finished the job.

How much does this cost?+

Our 4–6 week Diagnostic Assessment is fixed-fee (pricing shared after the first call). Productized builds — workflow automation, AI implementation, custom apps — are also fixed-fee and typically range $10k–$40k depending on scope. Managed retainers start at $3,500/month. You'll get an exact quote based on what the diagnostic uncovers.

Is my business data safe?+

We use enterprise-grade AI platforms (Claude for Work, ChatGPT Enterprise) that don't train on your data. For sensitive industries we can deploy fully within your existing Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace tenant — nothing leaves your environment.

What if AI changes and my systems break?+

That's exactly why our managed retainer exists. But even one-off engagements get documented, monitored, and maintainable systems — not fragile demos. We build to last.

Do you work on-site or remotely?+

Both. We work on-site with clients across the Southeast — Tennessee, Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, the Carolinas, and Florida — and remotely with clients anywhere in the U.S. For complex engagements, we usually do a mix.

Will automation replace jobs at my company?+

We build to augment, not replace. The goal is to take the work your team dreads — the copy-paste, the report-building, the status-update thread — and give them back time to do the work they were hired for. Every engagement we've run has ended with a more capable team, not a smaller one.
