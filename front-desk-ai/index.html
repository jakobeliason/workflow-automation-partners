What it handles

Every call. Every time. Every hour.

After hours

"Do you guys handle weekend emergencies?"

Qualifies the lead, looks up your weekend availability, books the appointment, sends a text confirmation. You wake up to a calendar full of real jobs.

Existing customers

"This is Linda. I need to reschedule my Thursday appointment."

Pulls Linda's record from your CRM, offers open slots, reschedules the visit, and texts the confirmation — without human intervention.

FAQ overflow

"How much for a kitchen install? Do you service Brentwood?"

Answers from your trained pricing and service-area data, then offers a free estimate slot before the caller hangs up.

Urgent escalation

"My pipe just burst — I need someone now."

Recognizes urgency, attempts to reach the on-call tech, texts you live, and books backup if no one picks up. No emergency slips through.
